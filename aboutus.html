Hello!
My name is Rose Worrall and I am a junior at Beacon High School. Having volunteered at an after-school program in Kingsbridge (KHCC) and worked multiple babysitting jobs, I have had a lot of experience with children. I have also volunteered at the SPCA animal shelter and fostered a family of cats. I currently play on multiple soccer teams and participate in my school's choir. In addition, I have been practicing piano for the past nine years. I love working with kids and I would like to help prevent the gender wage gap. I decided to help start this program because I love science. I have taken biology, chemistry, and am taking physics this year. I would love to give exciting lessons because science is more than memorizing the parts of a plant! I hope to provide a fun and educational journey to young girls.
